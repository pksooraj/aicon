Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline for website development varies depending on the complexity of the project. A simple website can be completed in 2-3 weeks, while more complex e-commerce sites may take 6-8 weeks. We'll provide you with a specific timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer customized pricing based on your specific needs. Our packages start at $1,500 for basic websites and increase based on functionality and features. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your requirements.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes, we offer monthly maintenance packages that include updates, security monitoring, backups, and content changes. Our maintenance plans start at $99/month depending on your website's needs.
