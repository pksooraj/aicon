Project Spotlight: Sunrise Cafe

A complete digital transformation for a local coffee shop

Project Overview

Sunrise Cafe needed a complete digital overhaul to attract more customers and streamline their ordering process. We created a responsive website with online ordering capabilities and implemented a social media strategy that increased their foot traffic by 35%.

Services Provided

  • Custom responsive website design
  • Online ordering system integration
  • Social media strategy and management
  • Local SEO optimization
  • Content creation and photography

Results

Within three months of launching their new website and implementing our social media strategy, Sunrise Cafe saw a 35% increase in foot traffic and a 28% increase in online orders. Their Instagram following grew by 150%, creating a strong community of loyal customers.
